I am Vijay Mathew, a photography enthusiast. Photography became a serious hobby for me during the first half of 2020. It has enabled me to see the life and landscape around my home in southern Kerala literally through new eyes!

Please send your comments and suggestions to: mathew.vijay@gmail.com. You may also request high resolution copies of my photographs by writing to the same email address. If you would like to buy high-quality prints of my photographs, please visit my ArtPal store.
